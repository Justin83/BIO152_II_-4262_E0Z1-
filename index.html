Watch & Study Pages
W1 · Ch. 22 ~15 min

Galápagos Finch Evolution

Natural selection and speciation from 40 years of Grants’ finch research on Daphne Major.
W2 · Ch. 23 ~17 min

Phylogenetic Tree of Anole Lizards

Anole adaptation, island speciation, and building a phylogenetic tree from DNA evidence.
W2 · Ch. 23 ~19 min

DNA Sequence Data & Models of Evolution

Molecular systematics: homology vs. homoplasy, parsimony, substitution models, and tree building.
W2 · Ch. 23 ~25 min Supplemental

Logic of Phylogenetics & Tree Construction

Deep-dive on the history and logic of phylogenetic trees. Optional past ~22:30.
W3 · Ch. 28 ~20 min

Diversity of Protists

Protist diversity: euglenids, diatoms, amoebas, green algae, parasites, and ciliates.
W3 · Ch. 28 ~5 min

The Secret Behind Bioluminescent Bays

Dinoflagellate bioluminescence: luciferin, luciferase, defense mechanisms, saxitoxin, and bay ecology.
W4 · Ch. 31 ~9 min

Fungi

Phylogeny, chitin/hyphae characteristics, heterotrophy, reproduction, and the five major phyla (A–B–C–G–Z).
