Why It Matters

Evolution is the central unifying theory of biology. Understanding natural selection, common descent, and the evidence for evolution will provide the conceptual foundation you need for everything else in BIO 152 — from taxonomy and biodiversity to ecology and community dynamics.

How to Navigate This Module

Begin with the Start Here materials to get oriented in the course. Then move into the evolution content. Work through the assigned readings and SmartBook activities before attempting quizzes or exams. Complete assignments in the order listed — each activity builds on the previous one.

All assignments are submitted through McGraw Hill Connect, accessed from Blackboard. Begin and end each session in Blackboard so your grades transfer correctly.

  • Complete all Start Here activities (About This Course, Syllabus review, Course Outline review).
  • Read the assigned textbook chapter(s) on evolution and natural selection.
  • Complete the SmartBook interactive reading assignment(s) in Connect, working until you reach the target score.
  • Complete any quantitative literacy assignments in Connect for this week.
  • Check the Course Outline for all due dates for this week.
