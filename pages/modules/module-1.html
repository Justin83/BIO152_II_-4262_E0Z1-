Why It Matters

Evolution by natural selection is the central unifying theory of biology. The concepts introduced this week — common descent, adaptation, speciation — will serve as the conceptual foundation for every topic that follows in BIO 152, from taxonomy and biodiversity to ecology and community dynamics. Starting here gives everything else context.
