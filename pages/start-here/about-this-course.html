Introduction

Welcome to BIO 152: Principles of Biology II. This online course presents knowledge of organismal, population, and community biology. It is the second semester in the BIO 150/BIO 152 two-semester sequence.

In this section, you will learn how the course is organized, how to navigate Blackboard, and how to reach me. You will also review key course tools and communication expectations.

Why it matters: This section covers the essential course information you will use from day one.

How you will use this: You will use this information to navigate the course, locate assignments, and submit your work on time.

Finding Work in the Class

Once you open a module, move through the pages in order by selecting the arrow on the right side of the module page. You can also use the arrow on the left to return to a previous page.

Each module includes information and activities that help you reach the week's objectives. It is important to go through each page in a module because many pages explain an assignment before you complete it. Do not skip directly to the graded activities.

You are required to access all course materials through Blackboard. Begin and end all assignments in Blackboard. Many assignments will transfer you to McGraw Hill Connect — when you finish, choose the option to return to Blackboard so your grades transfer properly.
