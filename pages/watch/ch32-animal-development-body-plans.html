What to Watch For

This is the visual anchor for Chapter 32. Focus on the core body-plan vocabulary: symmetry, blastula, gastrulation, blastopore, complete digestive tract, ectoderm, endoderm, and mesoderm.

Use the protostome/deuterostome explanation as a broad developmental comparison that sets up Chapter 33. The classic blastopore distinction is useful introductory language, but developmental patterns are not absolute rules for every animal lineage; Raven and SmartBook terminology remain the course standard.

The video is intentionally informal. Treat it as an overview that helps you visualize the body-plan concepts; use the Chapter 32 reading, PowerPoint, and SmartBook for the full course-level detail.
