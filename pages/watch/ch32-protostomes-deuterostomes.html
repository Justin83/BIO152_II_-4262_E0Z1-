What to Watch For

Use this as a short conceptual bridge between Chapter 32 body plans and Chapter 33 protostomes. The assigned segment is only about 2½ minutes.

Focus on the vocabulary: bilateral symmetry, blastula, mouth, anus, gut, mesoderm, and coelom.

The developmental patterns in the video are useful introductory comparisons, but they are not absolute rules for every animal lineage. Use the Raven chapter and SmartBook terminology as the course standard.
